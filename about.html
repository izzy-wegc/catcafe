About Izzy's Cat Cafe

Where Coffee Meets Compassion

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Izzy's Cat Cafe began with a simple dream: to create a space where homeless cats could find their forever families while people enjoyed delicious coffee and treats. Named after our founder who inspired this journey, we've since helped over 500 cats find loving homes.

What started as a small corner cafe has grown into a community hub where cat lovers gather, friendships bloom, and countless purrs fill the air. Every cup of coffee sold and every visit to our cat lounge directly supports our rescue efforts.

Our Mission

Rescue

We partner with local shelters to rescue cats in need, providing them with medical care, socialization, and a safe haven while they wait for adoption.

Rehabilitate

Our trained staff and volunteers work with each cat to address behavioral needs, build trust, and prepare them for life in a loving home.

Rehome

Through our unique cafe model, potential adopters can interact with cats in a relaxed environment, leading to better matches and lasting bonds.

Our Values

  • Compassion First: Every cat deserves love, patience, and a second chance
  • Community Building: Creating connections between people and cats
  • Transparency: Open adoption process and clear use of donations
  • Quality Care: Providing the highest standard of care for our feline friends
  • Sustainability: Building a self-sustaining model through our cafe operations

Adoption Process

1

Visit & Meet

Come to our cafe and spend time with our cats in the lounge area. Get to know their personalities!

2

Application

Found your perfect match? Fill out our adoption application and meet with our adoption counselor.

3

Home Check

We'll help ensure your home is ready and safe for your new family member.

4

Take Home

Once approved, take your new best friend home! We provide support and guidance for the transition.

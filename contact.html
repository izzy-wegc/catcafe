Directions & Parking

Getting Here

By Car: We're located on Whisker Lane between Main Street and Oak Avenue. Look for the pink cat sign!

Parking: Free parking available in our lot behind the building. Street parking also available on Whisker Lane (2-hour limit).

Public Transit: Take the Blue Line to Pawsville Station. We're a 5-minute walk north on Main Street, then right on Whisker Lane.

Accessibility

Izzy's Cat Cafe is fully wheelchair accessible with ramp access at the main entrance. Service animals are welcome. Please call ahead if you need any special accommodations.

Book Your Visit

Walk-ins are always welcome, but we recommend making a reservation during peak hours (weekends and evenings) to guarantee your spot in the cat lounge!

For groups of 6 or more, please call ahead: (555) 123-2287

Private events and birthday parties available! Email us at events@izzyscatcafe.com
